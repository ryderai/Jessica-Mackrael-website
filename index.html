About Jessica

The coast she calls home.
The name clients trust.

A Florida native with deep roots along the Panhandle, Jessica Mackrael pairs hometown knowledge with world-class representation — guiding buyers, sellers, and relocating military families from Boggy Bayou to the beaches of 30A.

For Jessica, real estate has never been about the transaction — it's about the trust behind it. Every client, from a first-time buyer to a family relocating on military orders, gets the same thing: her full attention, straight answers, and a plan built around what actually matters to them.

Real Clients, Real Moves

A few of the stories behind the sales.

Every deal is different. Here's a look at the kind of moves Jessica handles most — relocations, military PCS orders, and the transactions that don't go exactly by the book.

Military Relocation

Orders to Eglin AFB, Handled Start to Finish

A family with orders to Eglin needed to buy sight-unseen and close before report date. Jessica ran video walkthroughs, coordinated the VA loan timeline, and had them moved in with time to spare before their first day on base.

Long-Distance Move

Closing From Six States Away

An out-of-state buyer fell in love with a Destin listing from a phone screen. Jessica managed every showing, inspection, and negotiation remotely, keeping the buyer informed daily so the deal closed without a single in-person visit until move-in day.

Strategic Selling

Under Contract in Three Days

A seller needed to move fast ahead of a job transfer. Jessica repositioned the listing with updated pricing and staging guidance, and had it under contract in three days — well above asking, with a smooth path to closing.
