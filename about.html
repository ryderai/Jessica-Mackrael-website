Jessica Mackrael, Realtor®

Your trusted partner in Florida Panhandle real estate.

As a Florida native, Jessica has deep roots in the Sunshine State — and an unmatched understanding of the unique markets and communities along the Florida Panhandle, from the Alabama line to Panama City.

With nearly a decade of experience, she has become a top-producing Realtor known for excellence among clients and peers alike. But ask her, and she'll tell you her success isn't measured in closings — it's measured in clients who reach their goals with confidence, ease, and someone in their corner the whole way.

Jessica specializes in everything from beachside retreats and luxury waterfront homes to family residences and investment properties, with a client-first mentality and a Military Relocation Professional certification serving families moving to Eglin Air Force Base and Hurlburt Field. Whether it's a couple's first home or a family's fourth PCS move, she brings the same hands-on attention and local know-how to every deal.

Beyond the Closing Table

Why clients keep coming back.

Real estate on the Emerald Coast moves fast, and it's personal — this is where people raise families, retire, and put down roots near the base. Jessica treats every client relationship that way: not as a transaction to close, but as a homeowner's story she gets to be part of.
