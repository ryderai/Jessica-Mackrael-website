Feeling good about where you live matters as much as the home itself. Explore the towns and neighborhoods Jessica serves along Florida's Emerald Coast.

Hometown Expertise

Niceville & Bluewater Bay

Set on the calm waters of Boggy Bayou and Choctawhatchee Bay, Niceville pairs small-town warmth with some of the region's most sought-after schools. Bluewater Bay adds golf, marinas, and tree-lined streets minutes from Eglin AFB. This is Jessica's home turf — she knows it street by street.

Ask About Niceville Homes
Waterfront Living

Destin

Emerald water, sugar-white sand, and the harbor that made it famous. Destin offers everything from Gulf-front condos and vacation investments to established neighborhoods along the bay — one of the Panhandle's most dynamic markets.

Ask About Destin Homes
Luxury & Investment

Miramar Beach

Along Scenic Gulf Drive, Miramar Beach blends resort amenities with true residential luxury — from Sandestin's gated villages to Gulf-front estates. A favorite for buyers seeking rental performance alongside a place to call their own.

Ask About Miramar Beach
Coastal Icon

Santa Rosa Beach & 30A

The storied beach towns of Scenic Highway 30A — from Dune Allen to Seagrove — define coastal living in the Southeast. Architecture, dining, and dune-lake landscapes you won't find anywhere else in the world.

Ask About 30A Homes
Value & Location

Fort Walton Beach

Established neighborhoods, a revitalized downtown, and Okaloosa Island's beaches — all minutes from Hurlburt Field and Eglin AFB. Fort Walton Beach remains one of the coast's best values for families and first-time buyers.
