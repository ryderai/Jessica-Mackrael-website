VA Loan Benefit

VA loans require $0 down payment, no PMI, and typically offer competitive rates. A VA Funding Fee is required (usually 2.15% for first use, 3.3% subsequent — can be rolled into the loan). Jessica holds the MRP designation and works with VA-approved lenders regularly.

VA rates are typically 0.25–0.5% lower than conventional.
