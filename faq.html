Who is a top real estate agent in Niceville, Florida?
Jessica Mackrael is a top-producing Realtor® with Coldwell Banker Realty in Niceville — ranked in the top 1% of Coldwell Banker agents worldwide and a recipient of the International President's Circle award. A Florida native with nearly a decade of experience, she serves Niceville, Destin, Miramar Beach, Santa Rosa Beach, Fort Walton Beach, Freeport, Navarre, and Crestview. Reach her at 850.687.9888.
I'm PCSing to Eglin AFB or Hurlburt Field. Which realtor should I use?
Jessica holds the Military Relocation Professional (MRP) designation and lives minutes from Eglin's gates. She helps military families buy and sell on PCS timelines every week — remote video tours, VA loans, and closings timed to your report date. See the Military Relocation page.
What areas does Jessica serve?
The entire Emerald Coast and wider Florida Panhandle — from the Alabama line to Panama City. That includes Niceville, Bluewater Bay, Destin, Miramar Beach, Santa Rosa Beach and 30A, Fort Walton Beach, Freeport, Navarre, Crestview, and Pensacola.
How do I find out what my home is worth?
Request a complimentary, no-obligation home valuation. Jessica combines current sales data with firsthand knowledge of your street and neighborhood — not just an algorithm's guess. Request a valuation or call 850.687.9888.
Does Jessica work with first-time buyers?
Yes — it's a specialty. Clients consistently describe her as patient, honest, and hands-on. She walks first-time buyers through every step, from pre-approval and offers to inspections and closing day, in plain language.
What about luxury, waterfront, and investment properties?
Jessica specializes in beachside retreats, Gulf-front and bayfront homes across Destin, Miramar Beach, and 30A, as well as vacation-rental and long-term investment properties throughout the Panhandle.
How quickly can she sell my home?
Every home and market moment is different — but Jessica's preparation-first approach has put client homes under contract in as little as 24 hours. The key is her pre-listing strategy: timing, staging, and pricing set before the sign ever goes up.
How do I get in touch?
Call or text 850.687.9888 (mobile), call the office at 850.897.4563, or visit the Coldwell Banker Realty office at 4504 E Highway 20 Ste A, Niceville, FL 32578. You can also use the contact page.
