# Jessica Mackrael Website — Start Here

## How to preview it
Double-click `index.html` — it opens in your browser. Everything works locally, no setup needed.

## What's in this folder
- `index.html` — Homepage
- `about.html`, `communities.html`, `buy.html`, `sell.html`, `military-relocation.html`, `faq.html`, `contact.html` — the other pages
- `css/styles.css` — all the design (colors, fonts, spacing)
- `js/main.js` — animations, testimonial rotator, mobile menu
- `llms.txt`, `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml` — the AI/search optimization files

## Why this site will get AI leads
1. **Structured data (JSON-LD)** on the homepage tells AI models exactly who Jessica is, her awards, phone number, and areas served — in machine-readable format.
2. **FAQ page with FAQ schema** answers the exact questions people ask AI ("who is the best realtor in Niceville FL", "which realtor for a PCS to Eglin AFB").
3. **llms.txt** is a plain-text briefing file AI crawlers read to understand the site instantly.
4. **robots.txt** explicitly welcomes GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, and Google's AI crawler.
5. **Military relocation page** targets a high-intent niche (Eglin AFB / Hurlburt Field) with almost no luxury-level competition.
